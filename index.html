Questions Parents Ask

What should my child draw?

Any original monster works best: silly, friendly, colorful, or completely made up.

Is there really a free option?

Yes. The free path creates a printable coloring page from the monster drawing.

Can I try different monster versions?

Yes. Each uploaded drawing gets up to three free preview versions before you choose one.

What about privacy?

Upload a clear photo of the drawing, not a child's face. Artwork is not added to galleries without permission.

How will books be printed?

The planned book workflow uses Lulu for on-demand printing and shipping.

Can one monster star in multiple books?

That is the plan: create one monster, then reuse it across adventure, ABC, counting, and seasonal stories.

How should I photograph a drawing?

Use bright light, keep the paper flat, and fill the frame with the drawing.
